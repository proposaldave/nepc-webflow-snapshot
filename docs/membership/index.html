Book a CourtNew to Pickleball?

Not a member?
No problem.

New England Pickleball Club has lots of opportunities to play for our members and non-members. Non-members are welcome to book private courts or sign-up for social play up to 96 hours in advance.

Non-members can also sign up for lessons or classes 30 days in advance or sign up for our competitive leagues and ladders. Don’t let being a non-member stop you from making new friends and finding your best play at either of our clubs!

Membership FAQs

You’ve got questions, we’ve got answers. If our FAQs don’t address your question, just contact us and we’ll happily respond.

I want to join NEPC! How do I become a member?

That’s great! Membership is currently full but we’d love to have you join the waitlist so we know to reach out when we have an opening.

Can my membership be used at all NEPC locations?

Yes! All NEPC members are assigned a "home" club while also enjoying full member status at all of our club locations.

If you have a waitlist why don’t you just sell more memberships?

We cap our membership to ensure that our members have access to the times and programs that they want. We don’t want to oversell and have our members disappointed or frustrated that they can’t get in. This is why we welcome non-members to help fill in the gaps.

Do you offer seasonal memberships?

Yes, we offer summer memberships.
